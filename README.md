# Privacy Policy
Last updated: January 08, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes Our policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of Your information when You use the Service and tells You about Your privacy rights and how the law protects You.

## 1. Interpretation and Definitions
**Application** means the software program provided by the Company downloaded by You on any electronic device, named **Salah & Dhikr** (or "Namaz & Zikir").

**You** means the individual accessing or using the Service, or the company, or other legal entity on behalf of which such individual is accessing or using the Service, as applicable.

## 2. Collecting and Using Your Personal Data
### Types of Data Collected

#### Personal Data
We do **NOT** collect any personally identifiable information. The Application works entirely offline and does not require you to create an account or provide any personal details like your name, email address, or phone number.

#### Usage Data
We do **NOT** collect usage data. All data generated by the Application (such as your Dhikr counts, saved lists, and settings) is stored locally on Your device and is never transmitted to us or any third-party servers.

#### Location Data
Our Application requires access to your device's location solely for the following functional purposes:
- **Calculation of Prayer Times:** To provide accurate prayer times based on your geographical position.
- **Qibla Direction:** To calculate the direction of the Qibla (Kaaba) relative to your current location.

This location data is processed **locally on your device** in real-time. We do not store, track, share, or transmit your location history to any external server.

## 3. Children's Privacy
Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. Since our App does not collect any personal data at all, it is safe for users of all ages, including children.

## 4. Internet Access
The Application is designed to work offline. It does not communicate with any backend servers or APIs to store or fetch personal user data.

## 5. Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update Our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify You of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
